++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
                INSTALLATION
++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++

Move TheExpressor.vst3 to \Program Files\Common Files\VST3\
or your desired VST3 path.

In addtion, your system should have the latest Visual C++ Redistributable for x64:
https://docs.microsoft.com/en-US/cpp/windows/latest-supported-vc-redist?view=msvc-170#visual-studio-2015-2017-2019-and-2022
